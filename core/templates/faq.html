Q. What is COINFACE?
A. COINFACE - is a smart cloud mining and trading service developed for affordable cryptocurrencies mining and Forex trading, designed to provide frequent payouts within the shortest possible timeframe.

Q. What is cryptocurrency?
A. Cryptocurrency (or crypto currency) is a digital asset designed to work as a medium of exchange using cryptography to secure the transactions and to control the creation of additional units of the currency. Cryptocurrencies are classified as a subset of digital currencies and are also classified as a subset of alternative currencies and virtual currencies.

Q. Why should I invest with COINFACE?
A. COINFACE provides you a way to tap into the successful trading systems and therefore allowing you to diversify your trading portfolio whether you're an experienced trader or a newbie.

Q. How do I earn money by investing?
A. You are purchasing mining contracts based on mining hardware efficiency and costs. You will be receiving returns on weekly/monthly basis depending on amount of your contract.

Q. What influences my payouts?
A. Your payouts depends on the market price of Bitcoin and difficulty of mining and trading, as you will be receiving fixed payouts in Bitcoin.

Q. What are the conditions for profit withdrawal?
A. There are no conditions for withdrawal, however it is allowed to make 1 withdrawal per week.

Q. How long does it take to withdraw Bitcoin from my COINFACE Account?
A. Under normal circumstances, it can take up to 24 hours to process a Withdrawal as all user requests are audited for security purposes.

Q. Why up to 24 hours?
A. Withdrawal requests are processed during our office hours of between 09:00 - 17:00 GMT, Monday - Friday. Since Withdrawal Requests are queued for manual processing from when we receive them, there is no set time. In reality our team will bulk process requests several times a day, and more often than not, out of normal office hours; evenings and weekends. We stipulate that they will be processed within a period of 24 hours, taking into consideration time-zone differences for our clients globally.

Q. Can i withdraw my initial capital?
A. No, you can't withdraw initial capital within the investment contract; it will be released to you after 6 months (180 days). At the end of the investment contract of (6months) your initial capital can be withdrawn, after a 1.5% tax/closing/maintenance fees has been deducted. If an investor choose to close down account within the 6month lifetime, a two weeks notice should be given to the account manager, a 2% penalty is deducted and account will be closed in 72hours.

Q. How much profit do we earn with this program?
A. Up to 8% or 10% weekly for Bitcoin mining and up to 45% or 55% monthly profits for the Forex investment; depending on the contract package the individuals choose to buy into.

Q. Are the deposits and withdrawals done in BTC or fiat?
A. All transactions are done in BTC,ETH,LTC and BTCH mainly. But the profits are valued and calculated based on fiat rate, not the cryptos.

Q. I don't want to withdraw weekly, can i compound?

Your investment is held for 6months and payout is locked for that duration. For amounts below $10,000, there is no option yet for compounding.
